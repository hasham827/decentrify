Benefits

How you can Benefit

Decentrify targets both universities and employers. Universities can issue their student records on the blockchain and benefit from a reliable solution to the fake degrees problem. Employers on the other hand will have an integrated platform for all their degree validation checks for more informed hiring decisions.

Save time

We reduce degree validation time from 1-2 weeks to 10-15 seconds.

Save Money

Protect Reputation
